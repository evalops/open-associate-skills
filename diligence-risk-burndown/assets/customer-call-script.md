# Customer reference script (20 minutes)

## Context (30 sec)
- Thanks + confirm role + confirm they can speak openly.

## 1) Problem and trigger
- What triggered you to look for a solution?
- What did you do before?

## 2) Evaluation
- What alternatives did you consider?
- Why did you pick this vendor?

## 3) Deployment and time-to-value
- How long to first value?
- What was harder than expected?

## 4) Value and ROI
- What changed after adoption?
- What would make you renew / expand?
- What would make you churn?

## 5) Buying process
- Who approved budget?
- Any procurement/security blockers?

## Close
- If you were advising a friend, what would you warn them about?
