# Term sheet checklist

## Economics
- Price / valuation (pre, post)
- Round size
- Option pool (size; pre/post)
- Liquidation preference (multiple? participation? seniority?)
- Dividends
- Anti-dilution (broad/narrow-based weighted average? full ratchet?)
- Pro rata / follow-on
- Conversion rights

## Control
- Board composition + observer rights
- Protective provisions (veto list)
- Information rights
- Drag-along / voting agreements
- Founder vesting / acceleration
- Redemption rights (if any)

## Other
- Legal fees
- Side letters
- Most favored nation (MFN) clauses
