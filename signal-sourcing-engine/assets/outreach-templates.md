# Outreach templates

## Warm intro ask (operator → founder)
- Subject: Quick intro request: <founder/company> (specific wedge)
- Body:
  - Why them (1 sentence)
  - Why now (1 sentence)
  - The ask (15 min intro call)

## Cold outbound (thesis-driven)
- Subject: <specific wedge> + a concrete offer
- Body:
  - I’m tracking <wedge> because <why now>.
  - Your approach looks distinct because <specific evidence>.
  - If helpful, I can offer <intro/feedback/recruiting>.
  - Open to a 20-min chat next week?

## Follow-up (no response)
- One line: “If not a fit, who else in <space> should I be talking to?”
